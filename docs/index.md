## How it works

<div class="ebb-framed" markdown="1">

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Lzra2000/ProjectEbonHoldBuildAutomation/main/assets/how-it-works.svg" alt="How it works: define a build, automation acts on every choice screen, data is tracked, the Tuning Advisor suggests adjustments, and the loop repeats" width="100%">

Define a build with per-echo weights and policies. Autopilot evaluates every echo choice screen against it and acts for you. Every decision lands in the **Logbook** with its reasoning; the **Stats** views aggregate recorded runs; and the **Tuning Advisor** turns that evidence into concrete, reviewable suggestions.
